2. How We Handle Your Data

  • All photos and settings are stored locally on your device only and are never transmitted to external servers.
  • We do not collect, store, or process any personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.).
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.

4. Advertising

  • The app displays ads through Google AdMob.
  • AdMob may collect device identifiers (Advertising ID) for personalized advertising.
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads).
  • Users who purchase the "All-in-One" bundle will not see any ads.
  • Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. In-App Purchases

  • In-app purchases are processed entirely through Google Play Billing.
  • We do not collect or store payment information. All payment processing is handled by Google.
  • Google Play's Terms of Service: https://play.google.com/about/play-terms/

6. Camera & Storage Permissions

  • Camera: Required to capture photos with real-time filters. Camera data is processed on-device only.
  • Storage: Required to save captured photos to your device gallery.
  • No photos are uploaded to any server.

7. Your Rights

  • You can delete any saved photos through the app's gallery.
  • You can uninstall the app at any time to remove all local data.
  • You can reset your ad identifier through device settings.

8. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 14 years of age. The app does not require any personal information to operate.

10. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, we will notify users through in-app notice or app store update notes.
